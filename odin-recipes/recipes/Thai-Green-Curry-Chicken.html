Thai Green Curry Chicken

Instant Pot Thai-Style Green Curry Chicken

This Thai-style chicken has a creamy green coconut curry sauce with just the right amount of spice, and is made quickly and easily in your Instant Pot. It is delicious served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
  • ¼ cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons green curry paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil

Directions:

  1. Prepare Ingredients: Slice the chicken, bell pepper, and eggplant. Drain and slice the bamboo shoots. Prepare the lime by juicing it.
  2. Cook Chicken: Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium heat. Add the green curry paste and fry for about a minute until aromatic. Add the sliced chicken and stir until it's nearly cooked through.
  3. Add Vegetables: Add the diced eggplant and sliced bell peppers to the pan, cooking until they start to soften.
  4. Simmer with Coconut Milk: Pour in the coconut milk, bringing the mixture to a gentle boil. Add the bamboo shoots, fish sauce, sugar, and torn kaffir lime leaves if using. Reduce heat and let simmer for about 10 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is fully cooked.
  5. Finish and Serve: Stir in the Thai basil leaves and lime juice. Adjust seasoning with more fish sauce or sugar if needed. Serve hot with steamed jasmine rice.
