Ratatouille

A classic French dish, Ratatouille

Ratatouille can be served as a main dish with crusty bread, as a side to meats, or over a bed of cooked grains like quinoa or rice. It's also delicious both warm and at room temperature, making it versatile for any meal.

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant - 1 large, diced into 1-inch cubes
  • Zucchini - 2 medium, diced into 1-inch cubes
  • Bell peppers - 2 (one red, one yellow), diced into 1-inch pieces
  • Onion - 1 large, chopped
  • Tomatoes - 4 medium, roughly chopped
  • Garlic - 3 cloves, minced
  • Fresh thyme - 1 teaspoon
  • Fresh basil - 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Olive oil - 3 tablespoons
  • Salt - to taste
  • Black pepper - to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Vegetables: Wash and dice the eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes into similar-sized pieces. Chop the onion and mince the garlic.
  2. Saut√© Onions and Garlic: In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and minced garlic, cooking until the onions are translucent.
  3. Cook Eggplant: Add the diced eggplant to the pot, stirring occasionally, until slightly soft, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add Remaining Vegetables: Incorporate the zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes. Stir in the thyme, salt, and pepper.
  5. Simmer: Reduce heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally. The vegetables should be tender and the flavors well combined.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Finish by stirring in the fresh basil just before serving. Adjust seasoning if necessary.
